 ABOUT HASSAN ALLAM PROPERTIES [HAP]
HAP AT A GLANCE
Since its inception in the 1990’s, Hassan Allam Properties [HAP] has carved an enviable 
niche for itself as an exclusive boutique developer, building intimate communities where 
families always come ﬁrst.
Originating as a trusted family-run business passed down through three generations, 
HAP draws on 80 years of development expertise to master the equation of private luxury 
and inviting hospitality going beyond just building homes.
Driven by a community-centric vision and a passion for individuality, the company boasts 
a wide diversiﬁed portfolio of ﬁrst-class residential communities and vacation homes 
catering to families in search of exclusive experiences and private seaside getaways 
throughout prime locations in Egypt.
Today, HAP prides itself as a leading real-estate developer in partnering with leading local 
and global names in the industry to transcend local boundaries, bringing its unique 
success formula to the region by transforming inﬁnite landscapes into dream-like 
realities where families come together and homes come to life, while delivering the 
highest of standards that it's known for.

ABOUT HYDE P ARK
Hyde Park Developments is one of Egypt’s premier real estate companies founded in 2007 in hopes 
of catering to growing consumer demand for integrated residential communities. While new to the 
scene, HPD has grown to become one of the most respected developers in the real estate industry. 
In 2016, we went through a completely new overhaul with a new branding strategy to revamp our 
image. Previously known as Damac, the company was officially renamed Hyde Park Properties for 
Development, a jointly owned consortium of Egyptian investment and development companies that 
are well established in the local market. 
Our projects extend across the country. In the East, we have Hyde Park New Cairo, a fully integrated 
community equipped with Cairo’s largest park, a business district, and an extravagant venue for 
outdoor events, Hydeout. Going North you’ll find a coastal jewel we call Seashore, positioned on the 
coast of the Mediterranean. This is our latest venture into destinations by the sea offering homes 
with exceptional panoramic views. In the West, we offer a collection of boutique exclusive villas at 
Tawny, located by the new El Gezira Sporting club. We are also offering Garden lakes a new piece of 
land of 69 square meters in the most prime location in West Cairo.
We’ve worked diligently for our clients over the years, and while that has reaped its rewards, we are 
grateful that our efforts have been acknowledged by our industry peers. With an arsenal of 
internationally recognized architects, our company strives to meet all consumer demands and 
needs imaginable.

--- Page 3 ---
THE SHAREHOLDERS
Hyde Park is jointly owned by an esteemed consortium of Egyptian investment 
and development companies and institutions, all of which are well-established 
and highly credible in the local market. 
The company is held and managed by a strategic partnership and Ownership 
between Egypt’s Housing and Development Bank-HDB (36.9%), Egyptian Arab 
Bank-EBA (24%), The Holding Company for Investment and Development 
(18.2%), The New Urban Communities Authority-NUCA (20.88%), and El-Tameer 
for Investment & Real Estate Development S.A.E (0.02%).
